Supporting One Program: Delivering Lasting Impact

The Marquee Plan offers full platform access for performing arts students within a select program within a high school performing arts department, like band, choir, orchestra, or musical theatre—and administered by the specific related high school teacher and/or sponsoring booster club.

  1. Every 9th–12th grade performing arts student within denoted program
  2. High School Performing Fine Arts leadership managing targeted initiatives

Whether piloting the platform or focusing support on a high-impact ensemble, the Marquee Plan ensures tailored access, streamlined implementation, and meaningful results.

Cost to High School Program

Annual, one-time fee based on the number of select high school performing arts students enrolled (minimum 30 students):

  • $11.95 per high school (9th–12th) student in Non Title I schools
  • $1.95 per high school (9th–12th) student in Title I schools

Access to all platform features will be activated upon receipt of signed agreement and payment.
