Support Your Students: Strengthen Your Program

Engage, equip, and elevate—subscription plans designed for performing arts teachers and their students.

As a performing arts educator, your influence reaches far beyond rehearsals and performances. You help shape futures. You inspire young artists to dream bigger—and now, there’s a platform designed to support you in that mission.

Accoladi.com is a national digital platform that helps school districts, schools, and individual students navigate every stage of the college-bound journey—from the first spark of exploration to final acceptance letters. Whether your students plan to major in the arts or not, Accoladi gives them visibility, resources, and structured support to take the next step confidently.

Built by Experts. Backed by Experience.

Created by the trusted team behind ScholarshipAuditions.com, Accoladi was designed to meet the unique needs of performing arts students in band, choir, orchestra, and musical theatre. The platform empowers them to:

  • Build dynamic, searchable digital portfolios
  • Gain exposure to collegiate recruiters
  • Access guidance throughout the audition, scholarship, and application process

But most importantly—it supports you, their teacher, in making all of this happen

Two Dedicated Educator Portals
To serve both individual teachers and district-level leadership, Accoladi offers two exclusive educator portals:

  • AccoladiFineArtsDirector.com
    For district administrators, this portal provides large-scale student management tools, plan coordination, and visibility into student outcomes.
  • AccoladiTeacher.com
    Created specifically for classroom teachers, this portal offers ready-to-use instructional resources, curated articles, and over 100 “Teaching Moments” and pre-written blogs to bring college and career readiness into your classroom and to the parents of your students. As a high school performing arts teacher, you may also sponsor and manage a subscription plan for your students directly through this site.

Choose the Right Plan for Your Program
Every school and every program is different. That’s why Accoladi offers flexible subscription options to meet your needs:

For Teachers

  • Showcase Plan: Supporting all performing arts students in one specific high school.
  • Marquee Plan: Subscribe for one specific program in your high school, such as choir or band.
  • Individual Student Plans: Recommend students to subscribe independently through Accoladi.com.

For Districts
Accoladi also offers four comprehensive plans for district-wide implementation, available through AccoladiFineArtsDirector.com. Each plan includes tailored support, platform integration, and scalable management tools.

What Your Subscription Includes
With a Bravo or Marquee Plan, performing arts teachers gain access to powerful tools designed to make college and career readiness part of everyday teaching:

  • Upload and manage student rosters in bulk
  • Track progress through key college prep milestones
  • Access a library of curriculum-aligned Teaching Moments and blog content
  • Help students get discovered through recruiter-facing profiles
  • Enable secure, FERPA-compliant communication once a recruiter makes contact

It’s everything you need to bridge the gap between your program and their future.
