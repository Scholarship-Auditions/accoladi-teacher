Resources

At Accoladi, we believe that preparation isn’t just about practice—it’s about having the right tools, the right information, and the right strategy. That’s why our Resources section is designed to meet you at every stage of your journey, whether you're just beginning to explore your options or you're deep in the audition and application process.

From step-by-step guides that break down complex tasks to expert-written articles that offer real-world insights, our content is built for action and clarity. You’ll find curated directories of music and performing arts programs, how-to checklists for every milestone, scholarship search strategies, mock entrance exams, and performance prep tools—all developed to help you move forward with confidence.

No fluff. No guesswork. Just the resources you need to make informed decisions, stand out in auditions, and turn your passion into opportunity. Whether you're navigating college applications, building your artistic résumé, or exploring scholarship opportunities, everything you need is right here—organized, accessible, and designed with your success in mind.

A.J. Neubert's Journey

I dreamed big. I worked hard. By the time I was a senior at Kenmore East High School in Tonawanda, New York, I had built a strong résumé—filled with performances, hours of practice, and everything I thought I needed. But when it came to turning that effort into college opportunities, I didn’t know where to start. I had no roadmap. No connections. Just questions.

That’s when I found Accoladi. Through their platform, I was finally able to see what was out there—scholarships I’d never heard of, schools that matched my goals, and tools that helped me pull everything together. They even took my calls when I had questions, and that meant more than they know.

With Accoladi’s guidance, my hard work had direction. My dream had a plan. And I had a way forward.

Today, I’m proud to say I studied at both Oberlin Conservatory and the Colburn School—two of the most respected music institutions in the country.
Yes, dreams do come true. And for me, Accoladi helped make that possible.

A.J. Neubert

Principal Bassoonist - Charlotte Symphony
