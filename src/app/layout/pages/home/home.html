A.J. Neubert's Journey

Despite having a strong musical résumé, A.J. struggled to find college opportunities that matched his talent and aspirations. That's when Accoladi stepped in, opening doors he never thought possible.

Through our platform, A.J. discovered scholarships, connected with prestigious institutions like Oberlin Conservatory and the Colburn School, and received invaluable guidance throughout his application process.

Today, A.J. serves as Principal Bassoonist with the Charlotte Symphony, living proof that with the right support, dreams do come true. And for him, Accoladi helped make that possible.
