About Accoladi

At Accoladi, we believe every performing arts student deserves the chance to turn their talent into a future—whether they major in music or simply carry their passion with them into college, the military, or a chosen career path.

Since 2018, Accoladi has been the trusted online hub for students pursuing higher education or the military through the arts. We've helped thousands of performers, future educators—majors and non-majors alike—connect with colleges, universities, conservatories, and scholarship opportunities tailored to their unique skills and aspirations.

What We Offer Students and Families

  • Step-by-step college guidance—from middle school to senior year
  • Tools to boost audition success and stand out to college recruiters
  • A secure, professional profile platform for showcasing talent
  • Access to both institutional and non-institutional scholarships, including many that are underutilized or unknown

We support every musical path—whether it leads through marching band, jazz combo, musical theatre, classical voice, show choir, or contemporary songwriting. Our goal is to make sure every aspiring collegiate performer gets noticed, accepted, and funded.

For Parents: You Don’t Have to Navigate This Alone

Accoladi is more than a resource—it’s a guide. We equip families with the knowledge, strategy, and confidence to pursue college through the performing arts. We help you:

  • Understand audition and application timelines
  • Identify scholarship opportunities early
  • Track progress and milestones through a secure dashboard
  • Connect directly with real college faculty and recruiters

We mentor students and parents starting in middle school, because preparation matters—and opportunity grows with time.

We Make Introductions That Change Lives

With relationships across more than 35,000 college and military music professionals, we actively promote our student performers to decision-makers: deans, professors, directors, and recruiters. We don’t just offer information—we create access.

Our Board of Directors and Leadership Team are visionaries who live and breathe Accoladi's mission. Through their collaborative and creative guidance, they keep Accoladi innovative and responsive to the evolving needs of our performing arts students in today's ever-changing collegiate landscape.

PARTNERSHIP

Accoladi recognizes the vital role music educators and collegiate recruiters play in guiding students and building opportunities. We understand that when educators and recruiters work together, students are empowered to pursue their dreams with clarity and confidence. Our tools are designed to strengthen these connections and streamline the path from classroom to the college stage. That's why we've created dedicated platforms to support each of these essential partners.

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion

At Accoladi, we are committed to making higher education in the performing arts accessible, inclusive, and welcoming to all. We believe that every student—regardless of their background or identity—deserves the opportunity to be seen, heard, and supported on their artistic, collegiate, or military journey.

Our platform is designed to be a secure and affirming space for performers of all races, ethnicities, nationalities, gender identities and expressions, sexual orientations, faiths, income levels, abilities, and ages. We are continually listening, learning, and adapting—not just to be inclusive, but to actively celebrate the diversity that strengthens our community of student performing artists and the parents and educators who support them.

Because talent knows no boundaries—and neither should opportunity.
